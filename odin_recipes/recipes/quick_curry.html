Quick Curry

Description

Ingredients

Serves: 3-4

Prep Time: 30min

This is an easy-to-prepare and healthy addition to your dinner repertoire. Of course, it is possible to add chicken to the recipe if desired but I believe you’ll find this dish is still rather filling as a vegetarian option.

  • Red curry paste, 2 tablespoons
  • Carrots, 3-4
  • Large white onion, 1
  • White sugar, about 1/4 cup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Canola oil, about a Tablespoon
  • Garlic, 2 cloves
  • Parsley, about 4-5 sprigs
  • Coconut milk
  • Soy sauce, 1/4 cup

Instructions

  1. First, let’s heat up the curry paste in the canola oil.
  2. Next, let’s dice the onions and garlic and sautee them with the curry paste over low-medium heat.
  3. While those are softening up, chop the red pepper, carrots (after you peel them), zucchini, and brocolli and add them to the sautee.
  4. Once the base veggies are softened, we’re ready to add the soy sauce and sugar. Let it cook with the veggies for 10 minutes.
  5. Once the soy sauce and sugar are incorporated, add the coconut milk and let in cook for 2 minutes.
  6. The curry should be a dark orange, burgundy color at this point.
  7. Remove from heat and add cilantro.
  8. Serve with rice and a lime!
